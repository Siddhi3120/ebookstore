An alien craft has crashed in the Antarctic. A creature has crawled from the wreckage and become frozen in ice. The men of Fafnir Station have transported it back to their base. Some of the men can hear it whispering in their mind. Some are afraid and want to destroy it... ...and some will kill to defend it.

In this nerve shredding sci-fi thriller, Lee McGeorge has fused the visceral horror of John Carpenter’s seminal movie with the psychological elements of Campbell’s, Who Goes There?
